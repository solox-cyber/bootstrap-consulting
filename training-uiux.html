With new Software applications being churned out every now and again, Application Development teams, need UI/ UX designers, just as much as they need Business Analysts.
Whilst the Business Analyst elicits the software requirements and comes up with process models, the UI / UX designer is relied upon to come up with prototypes of the application to be developed.
This gives the stakeholders an opportunity to sign-off, based on the prototype and also gives the developers a clearer idea of what they are expected to develop.
The Program Culminates in a Virtual Capstone Internship. The work experience program replicates work at top companies, and connects you to the companies themselves.

Who should attend:

Intending UI/ UX Professionals.

Duration: 20 Hours for the Online Live Sessions (Self-Paced, Virtual Capstone Internship, follows).

Fee: $280 (or N120, 000) Financing Option

(Connection details to the virtual class will be sent after Registration, and a Certificate of Participation will be issued upon Completion of the Training and Virtual Capstone Internship).

Course Outline:

  • Introduction to Design thinking
  • Information Architecture (Site and App Maps)
  • Interaction Design (Flows, Wireframes, Prototyping Tools).
