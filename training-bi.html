Decision makers in organizations today, rely on insights from data, to make their decisions. This data may or may not reside on different platforms (Souces).

Business Intelligence, is essentially, concerned with presenting data in such a way, that it provides actionable insights; based on the metrics that are of importance to the decision makers.

This program will equip you with the skills required to start you off in your journey as a Business Intelligence Analyst, by taking you on knowledge excursion on the use of Power BI, which is a user-friendly software application.

The Program Culminates in a Virtual Capstone Internship. The work experience program replicates work at top companies, and connects you to the companies themselves.

Who should attend:

Intending Business Intelligence Professionals, IT Business Analysts, Functional Consultants, Business Users.

Duration: 20 Hours for the Online Live Sessions (Self-Paced, Virtual Capstone Internship, follows).

Fee: $280 (or N120, 000) Financing Option

(Connection details to the virtual class will be sent after Registration, and a Certificate of Participation will be issued upon Completion of the Training and Virtual Capstone Internship).
