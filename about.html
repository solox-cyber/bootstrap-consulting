About US

Bootstrap Consulting is an IT Training and Consulting Firm committed to delivering the best standards of training and consulting. We recognize the importance of Digital Transformation in Industries, today, and are positioned to assist Companies with their Digital Transformation initiatives, whilst also focusing on the capacity development of professionals in that space. Established in 2013, Bootstrap Consulting remains committed to creating a platform for recursive development by providing Training and Consulting Services, at highest industry-standards while adding value to our clients; which can be measured by increased performance on-the-job, and increased returns on investment.

Vision

To be a major player in the advancement of technology, through Training and Consulting.

The 'How'

Whilst we are mindful of the constantly changing global ICT trends, we ensure that our curricula is constantly updated in line with what is globally obtainable. We not only ensure that we create a platform for recursive development through our training, but we are also positioned to assist organizations in their Digital Transformation Journeys, through Business Analysis and Project Management; to the end that they derive optimum returns on their Technology Investments.

We Believe That:

  • The more ICT aware people are, the more productive and globally relevant they will be; seeing that Information and communication technologies have found their way into our everyday lives; from daily communication to transacting business e.t.c.
  • The more ICT aware people are, the more employable they will be; especially since ICT drives most businesses today.
  • This will reduce the rate of unemployment and also reduce the skills-shortages in certain fields. Organizations can use all the assistance they can get, as they embrace technology; whilst they focus on their actual business operations.

Core Values:

  • Excellence
  • Integrity
  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Team Work
