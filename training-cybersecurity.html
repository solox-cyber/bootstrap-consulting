It has become very critical to develop Expertise on how to identify potential Information security breaches, detect breaches when they occur in a timely manner, remedy the breaches, and develop mechanism against future similar occurrences. Global losses from cyber crime Skyrocketed to nearly 1 Trillion in 2020 (Washington Post). Also, the global shortfall of Cyber security resource persons, stood at 3.12m, in Nov. 2020 (According to (ISC)2 study)).

As an Authorized CompTIA Delivery Partner, we therefore, offer In-depth Training to close this skill-gaps; equipping technical personnel with the most relevant Information on how to identify, contain, eradicate and learn from security Incidents within the organization.

This Cyber Security Program bundle includes:

Our Cyber Security Program Culminates in a Virtual Capstone Internship (Curated by PWC). The work experience program replicates work at the company

In addition, Post-training Apprenticeships are available to UK Citizens; who are living in the UK

Who should attend:

Intending Cyber Security Professionals, IT Support/ Help Desk Officers desiring to pivot their careers into Cyber Security, as well as Cyber Security Professionals who wish up-skill.

Duration: 20 Hours for the Online Live Sessions (Self-Paced, Virtual Capstone Internship, follows).

Start Date: 2nd April, 2022. (Schedule can be adjusted to suit participants who are combining this with work, if the majority fall in this category).

Fee: $280 (or N120, 000). Financing Option

(Connection details to the virtual class will be sent after Registration, and a Certificate of Participation will be issued upon Completion of the Training and Virtual Capstone Internship).

Course Outline:

  • Introduction to Cyber Security
  • Attacks, Threats and Vulnerabilities (Includes attacks, threats and vulnerabilities from IoT and embedded devices, newer DDoS attacks and social engineering).
  • Architecture and Design (Includes coverage of enterprise environments and reliance on the cloud, which is growing quickly as organizations transition to hybrid networks (on-premises and cloud)).
  • Implementation (Includes a focus on administering identity, access management, basic cryptography, PKI, wireless and end-to-end security).
  • Operations and Incidence Response (Includes organizational security assessments and incident response procedures, such as detection, mitigation and basic digital forensics of incidents).
  • An Overview of Governance, Risk and Compliance (Includes how to support basic organizational risk management, security controls and teamwork to support regulations such as PCI-DSS, SOX, HIPAA, GDPR, NIST and CCPA).
